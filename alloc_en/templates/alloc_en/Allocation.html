Instructions Part {{ Constants.part_alloc }}.
This is the last part of the experiment and is played for a single round. In this round, you will be endowed with {{ Constants.pie }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} and you have to decide how you want to distribute the points between two players who have been randomly matched with you: Players A and B.

Player A is one of the players assigned to group ● "{{ circles_label }}" in Parts {{ Constants.part_name }} and {{ Constants.part_fixed }}. Player B is one of the players assigned to group ▲ "{{ triangles_label }}" in Parts {{ Constants.part_name }} and {{ Constants.part_fixed }}.

The computer will randomly choose the allocation decision of one of the {{ Constants.players }} players who have participated together in the experiment, and will implement the payment of that decision. For instance, if your decision is chosen, players A and B matched with you will receive the points you have allocated to them. None of the other 5 players (including yourself) will receive any points in this part. Similarly, if another player's decision is chosen and you are one of the players A or B matched with him, you will receive the points allocated to you.
