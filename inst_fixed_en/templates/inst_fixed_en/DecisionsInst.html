Groups, Appearances & Labels
In Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }} you will participate with {{ Constants.others }} other players (the same as in Part {{ Constants.part_name }}). Each player is randomly assigned to a group ● "{{ circles_label }}" or ▲ "{{ triangles_label }}" (inner symbol) , and will also have an appearance ◯ or △ (símbolo externo). Each player will also have a numeric label from 1 to {{ names }}. You will see the group, appearance and label of each player on the screen (see Figure 1).

On the header of each screen you will see a summary of this information as in the example below:


In each round there will be {{ Constants.total_circles }} players in group ● "{{ circles_label }}" and {{ Constants.total_triangles }} in group ▲ "{{ triangles_label }}". A player’s group and appearance will not change between rounds during Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }}, hile his numeric label and position in the screen will be randomly changed in each round.

For instance, the player in the header above is in group ● "{{ circles_label }}" and has the numeric label 7 in round 1. In round 2, the same player will be in the same group than before but his label may be any number from 1 to {{ names }}, and his position may be anywhere on the screen. The same is the case for all other players.

Stage 1: Connections
In the Connections stage you can propose a connection to those players you want to participate with in Stage 2. You can propose a connection to any player no matter their group or appearance. If you propose a connection to a player who also proposes to you, there is an active connection between the two of you. In Stage 2 you will only participate with the players with whom you have an active connection.

You will see your connections on the screen (see Figure 2). There is a light arrow leaving from you for each connection you proposed, and a dark arrow heading towards you for each connection proposed to you. If both arrows are present you have an active connection with that other player. If only one of you proposes a connection but the other does not, there is no active connection between the two of you. *Your connections are illustrated with thicker lines and those of others are thinner.

Stage 2: Actions
In the Actions stage you will pick one of two actions: or . You only pick one action for the stage, not one for each connection. You will get {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each of your active connections choosing the same action as you. You will not get any {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for an active connection choosing a different action than you, nor from a player choosing the same action as you, if he is not an active connection.

The action chosen by each player is illustrated by the color of his appearance (see Figure 3).
