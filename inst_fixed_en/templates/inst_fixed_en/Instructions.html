Groups, Appearances & Labels

In Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }} you will interact with {{ Constants.others }} other players. At the beginning of the experiment each of you was assigned to one of two groups: ● "{{ circles_label }}" or ▲ "{{ triangles_label }}" (internal symbol), and was assigned an appearance: ◯ or △ (external symbol) and a numeric label (between 1 and 7 {{ names }}). Your group and appearance are fixed for all {{ Constants.rounds_fixed }} rounds in Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }} while your numeric label and your position on the screen will change in each round.
There are 3 stages in each round..

Stage 1: Connections

In stage 1, you can propose connections to the other players. A connection with another player is active if both you and the other player propose a connection to each other. Each connection you propose will cost you {{ Constants.link_cost }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }}, independently of whether the other player also proposes a connection to you or not.

Stage 2: Actions

In stage 2, you will choose an action: or . You will earn {{ Constants.exp_currency }} depending on your group, the action you choose, and the number of your active connections choosing the same action as you:

If your group is ● "{{ circles_label }}" and you:

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection also choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection also choosing

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection also choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection also choosing

If your group is ▲ "{{ triangles_label }}" and you:

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection also choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection also choosing

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection also choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection also choosing

Stage 3: Results

In stage 3, you will see how many {{ Constants.exp_currency }} you get in that round. The {{ Constants.exp_currency }} are calculated by the gains of coordinating with your active connections minus the costs of your proposed connections. The border of each player's appearance (◯ or △) shows the action that player chose.

Earnings in Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }}

You will participate for {{ Constants.rounds_fixed }} rounds in Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }}. At the end of the experiment the computer will randomly select one of the {{ Constants.rounds_fixed }} rounds. The number of {{ Constants.exp_currency }} you got in the selected round will be added to the total number of points used to determine your payment.
